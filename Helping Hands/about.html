Welcome to HELPING Hands

Helping Hands is an NPO that is based in the Township of Khayelitsha, We simply bring the startegy of minimizing crime and unemployment rate.
Khayelithsa is the second lagest Township in South Africa, and about 90% of people who are living in Khayelithsa are from the small Villages situated in the Eastern Cape province because of hoping to find better oppotunities for a better living.
The rate of crime & unemployment is very high in Khayelithsa. Due to a very high rate of unemployment in the Township, most of people are accepting any opportunity that comes to them (whether good or bad) as way of earning something for survival. In most cases there are no opportunities, now some people think of crime(Breakins, Robberies, Car highjaking etc) as a solution to their problem and result in the increasing of crime.
